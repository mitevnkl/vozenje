Our Privacy Policy

We understand the importance of protecting personal information and take the privacy of our users seriously.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information we receive when you use our ride-sharing app.

Information Collection and Use

When you sign up for our app, we will collect certain personal information from your Facebook account, including your name, email address, and profile picture.

We use this information to create your user account and to establish trust among our users on the platform..

When you create a post for ride sharing, you will be asked to provide information about the ride, such as the destination, date and time, and number of seats.

This information will be visible to other users who are looking for a ride and may match with your post.

When you see a match for your post, you will be able to see the match's Facebook information, including their name and profile picture.

We will use the information you provide to match you with other users, initialize trust, and to facilitate the ride-sharing process.

Information Retention

The post will be self-destroyed when the post's end_time parameter has past.

You can also destroy the post at any time.

We will retain the information you provide for as long as necessary to provide our services and to comply with legal obligations.

Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect the information we receive from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
